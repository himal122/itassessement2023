Space Planning

A key step in the interior design process is space planning. It is done with a thorough investigation of how space is used to fulfil each area's function. The circulation patterns that depict how people will move across the area will also be specified in the space plan. The furniture placement and activities that will take place in each zone are defined by the designer. It entails drawing out a space plan, which depicts how functional pieces are arranged within a space.

Interior architecture

Interior architecture is the process of designing a structure or shelter from the inside out or creating a fresh interior for a particular kind of fixable dwelling. In terms of architecture, interior architecture is the design of an interior. It involves using and reusing the area. It is the method used to create interiors that take into account every feature of the house and how it is used.
